Submit Your Research Proposal

Have a research idea that aligns with our mission? Submit your proposal to BVP and let us evaluate whether we can provide funding and support for your scholarly endeavor.

Submission Guidelines

Research Relevance

Your proposal should align with Indian classical studies, Sanskrit literature, philosophy, or related interdisciplinary fields.

Academic Merit

Demonstrate clear research objectives, methodology, and potential contribution to the field of knowledge.

Community Impact

Show how your research will benefit the academic community and preserve or advance cultural knowledge.

Timeline & Feasibility

Provide realistic timelines and demonstrate the feasibility of your research proposal.
